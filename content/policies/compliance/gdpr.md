## Overview

As a Data Processer, {{ config.extra.organization }} has established the following processes to ensure that {{ config.extra.organization }} is fully compliant with GDPR regulations. Here we outline the processes that we have in place to accommodate the following activities:

## Deletion Of Customer Data

If you ask to delete a personal account with {{ config.extra.organization }}, {{ config.extra.organization }} will initiate deletion of this data after 30 days. Please note there may be some latency in deleting this data from our servers and storage and we may retain this data if necessary, to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes or enforce {{ config.extra.organization }}' agreements.

## Customer Deletion Of User Data

You have control over your personal data and how we collect, use and share personal data that you have provided to us. You can download a copy of your personal data or request a copy of the personal data you provided to {{ config.extra.organization }} by contacting us a [support@passasgeways.com](mailto:support@passasgeways.com)

{{ config.extra.organization }} permits administrator accounts, administrators can create the following:

- Organisation - Member accounts

- Organisation - Administrator accounts

- Post content

Administrator can also perform the following tasks:

- Assign organisation user roles

- Add and remove organisation users

- Edit a user's profile (after the user authorises the edit);

- Send password-reset instructions

- Disable organisation users

Once an administrator disables a user, the user's access to content is disables immediately across the platform.

## Turn Off Tracking So Data Subjects Can Be Forgotten

We do not engage in the collection of personal data about your online activities outside of the {{ config.extra.organization }} websites, and we do not allow third parties to collect personal data from {{ config.extra.organization }} websites as part of their tracking of such online activities. Accordingly, we do not currently process or comply with any web browser's "do not track" signal or other mechanisms that provide consumers with the ability to exercise choice regarding the collection of personal data about online activities over time and across third-party websites

## Encryption Of Customer Data

{{ config.extra.organization }} will take commercially reasonable security measures consistent with Industry Standards, to protect against unauthorised access to Posted Content and to maintain the digital integrity of Posted Content. Usage of {{ config.extra.organization }} may be controlled and monitored by {{ config.extra.organization }} for security purposes.

Data is encrypted in-transit via RSA 4096-bit encryption and at rest on devices through the native applications via AES-256. TLS >= 1.2 is enforced strictly.

## Authorization Of Access To Confidential Customer Data

Access must be authorised except in very limited circumstances. Only designated employees with the explicit written permission of the customer, through contracts, purchase agreements, emails, or some other means whereby the identification of the {{ config.extra.organization }} customer is clear and the intention of the customer to grant {{ config.extra.organization }} representatives access is explicit, may access confidential customer data. If an employee accesses or discloses confidential customer data without the customer's written authorisation or without a job-related reason for doing so, the employee violates {{ config.extra.organization }}' policy and may be subject to termination. In such a circumstance, the {{ config.extra.organization }} Incident Response Plan shall be executed.

## Security Controls

We encrypt and store customers' at-rest data in one of our physical data centres. We select the data centre that is closest to the customer's location, and that is the only place where the data will reside. For example, data belonging to a UK customer is encrypted and stored in the UK data centres only. Data does not cross borders to any of our other data centres

## Export Mechanisms

At any time during the term of the contracted service, customers can export their Posted Content from {{ config.extra.organization }}. Customers may also negotiate arrangements with {{ config.extra.organization }} to receive their Posted Content.

## Opt-Out

All emails will contain links allowing you to opt-out of receiving such data. In addition, you may opt out of receiving materials or other communications from us and our affiliates by contacting us via email at [help@{{ config.extra.organization }}meetings.com](mailto:help@{{ config.extra.organization }}meetings.com). For recipients in the EU, your consent to receive email from us will be obtained.
